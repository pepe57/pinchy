---
title: AI Disclosure
description: Pinchy labels every agent as an AI assistant in the chat header, so users always know they are interacting with an AI system.
---

Pinchy shows a small **AI assistant** badge in the chat header next to every agent's name. The badge is always present — on desktop and mobile — so a user opening a chat sees immediately that they are interacting with an AI system, not a human.

## Why

EU AI Act Article 50 requires that people be informed when they interact with an AI system (unless the context makes it obvious). A persistent header label is the simplest, most durable form of that disclosure: it does not depend on a one-time system message the user might dismiss or scroll past, and it stays visible across every conversation and channel.

## What the badge says

The badge reads **AI assistant** and carries a tooltip: _"You are chatting with an AI assistant."_ Every Pinchy agent is an AI assistant, so the label is always accurate — there is nothing to configure per agent in v1.

:::note
The disclosure applies to the Pinchy web chat. Messages delivered through Telegram or other channels are subject to that channel's own presentation; the badge is shown in the Pinchy UI.
:::
